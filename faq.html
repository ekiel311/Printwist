Frequently Asked Questions
What is print-on-demand (POD) for clothing?
It's like magic, but with clothes! We print your designs on clothing items only when someone places an order. No magic wand required.
What types of clothing can I customize through your print-on-demand service?
You name it, we print it! T-shirts, hoodies, caps, and more. If you can wear it, we can print it.
What printing methods do you use for custom clothing design?
We use high-tech printing dwarves that work day and night to ensure your designs look fabulous. Just kidding, it's digital and screen printing!
What file formats do you accept for design uploads?
We speak geek fluently! We accept formats like JPG, PNG, and even PDF. No need to learn the language of the tech-masters. Just make sure its in high resolution and quality format.
Is there a minimum order quantity for custom clothing prints?
Nope, we're all about freedom here. Order one or order a thousand. it's your call.
Do you offer nationwide shipping for custom clothing orders in the Philippines?
Yup! From Luzon to Mindanao, we've got your back. Whether you're in the heart of Manila or on a beautiful beach in Boracay, your custom clothing is just a click away.
What is the pricing structure for your print-on-demand service?
It's simple: the more you order, the less you pay. We make it rain discounts when you go big!
Can I use your service to create my own clothing brand?
Absolutely! With us, you can turn your creative vision into a local clothing sensation right here in the Philippines. Time to make your mark in the local fashion scene!
How do I track the status of my custom clothing order?
Tracking is as easy as pie (we prefer eggpie, by the way). We'll send you updates faster than you can say "where's my order?"
Do you offer bulk discounts for large custom clothing orders?
Yes we do. The more you order, the more you save. We're all about rewarding your fashion ambition with discounts that make your wallet smile.
